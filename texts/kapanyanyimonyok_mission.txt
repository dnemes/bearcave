The Kapanyányimonyók smiles at you approvingly, and gestures that you should
sit down. You squat down onto the floor, and he starts to speak.
'There is a door behind you, which leads to the Room of the Bear. There you
will find a bear. The bear is guarding a jar of special honey, which I need
for my mush. You'r gonna bring it to me.' he says, and you stand up, and turn
for the door.
